BookShelf is an app for book lovers where they can find and post reviews on their favourite books, connect with people having similar taste and share books with them.

The features include
Posting reviews:
Click on the book you want to post a review on, it will take you to the book profile page. There you will find an "add" button in the lower right corner for posting reviews.

Finding Books:
There is an option called "All Books" in the navigation drawer where you can find all books stored in the database. You can also search for books in the homepage.

Make your own books avilable to borrow:
If you have a copy of a book and want to share it with your friends,find that book in the app and hit the "Add to Booklist" button for making it available for your friends to borrow.

Make a list of the books you wish to read in future:
There is an "Add to Wishlist" button in each book profile for making a wishlist of your own.

Find out if someone near you has the book you want to borrow & send them a request for it:
There is a "Check Availability" button in each book profile that shows you the loactions of the people who have that book. You can click on their location, go to their profile & send a request for borrowing the book.

Managing book requests:
If you send someone a book request, it will get shown in the "Requested Books" option in your navigation drawer. You can check if the person you sent a request to has approved the book for you to borrow. After you've confirmed that you've recieved the book, it will be added to your "Borrowed Books" option in the navigation drawer & remain there until you return it & the book owner confirms that he has recieved it. The same approach is used if someone sends you a request for a book. It gets shown in the "Requests" option until you approve it to lend & is added to "Lent Books" option until the request sender return it to you.

Send a book request if it doesn't exist in the database:
You can request for adding a book in the "Request for a book" option.
